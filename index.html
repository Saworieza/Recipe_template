Green Beans With Cherry Tomatoes

Download recipe
Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 pounds green beans, trimmed and cut into 2 inch pieces.
  • 1 1/2 cups water.
  • 1/4 butter.
  • 1 tablespoon sugar.
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt.
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper.
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh basil.
  • 2 cups cherry tomato halves.
    • More Ingredients

Difficulty level Level 1

Place beans and water in a large saucepan. Cover and bring to a boil. Set heat to low, and simmer until tender, about 10 minutes. Drain off water, and set aside.

Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in sugar, garlic salt, pepper and basil. Add tomatoes and cook stirring gently until soft. Pour the tomato mixture over the green beans, and toss gently to blend

Broccoli And Cheese Casserole

Download recipe
Ingredients
  • 3 cups water.
  • 1 (10 ounce) package yellow rice.
  • 1/4 butter.
  • 1 (16 ounce) broccoli.
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar process cheese sauce.
    • More Ingredients

Difficulty level Level 1

In a saucepan bring water to a boil. Add rice, reduce heat cover and simmer for 20 minutes.

While the rice is cooking, place the broccoli in a large skillet. Heat stove to low-medium . Add processed cheese sauce to the broccoli and let melt. When the rice has completed cooking add broccoli-cheese mixture and rice to 9x13 baking dish and mix well.

Place casserole in the preheated 400 degress F(200 degrees C) oven for 10 minutes; or until heated through.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese.
  • 1 teaspoon dry salad dressing mix.
  • 1 teaspoon onion power.
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder.

Difficulty level Level 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).

Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water just below the bottom of the steamer. Bring water to a boil. Add cauliflower, cover and steam untill very tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Transfer cauliflower to a bowl, mash and strain excess water.

Mix cauliflower, sour cream, Cheddar cheese, ranch dressing mix, onion powder, and garlic powder together in a 9-inch bakig dish; top with butter.

bake in the pre-heated oven until bubbling, 30 45 minute.
